About Us

Our Beginnings

Owners Tom & Tina Beck have worked very hard throughout the years acquiring the experience & skills that have resulted in their continued success in the construction field of business. From humble beginnings as a roofing contractor to home designer/builder to general construction remodeling & renovations, Tom & Tina have operated both large & small operations from a few employees to nearly 100 employees during the boom of the 1990’s. Over the past three decades, Tom & Tina’s repertoire of experience includes performing general construction, as well as various specific trade services within residential, commercial & government aspects, including the Department of Defense, Department of Interior, as well large & small private & public apartment/condominium developments, custom homes & semi-customs homes.


What we do now

These days, to ensure & maintain unparalleled quality control, all work is performed under the direct supervision & scrutiny of the owner, Tom Beck, be it a simple re-roof to a complete home restoration or custom build. Each project is a reflection on the abilities of T. A. Beck Construction, whereas, most projects are obtained primarily through personal references of a previous client. The fact the majority of the work is performed by our in-house staff of qualified craftsmen, as well as long-established relationships with specialty trade contractors, our ability to provide in-house architectural design & Toms’ 30 years of authentic experience, affords our clients exceptional value/costs savings. The Beck’s mantra has always been to provide prompt, quality work at reasonable prices. That’s it – a simple no-nonsense approach.
